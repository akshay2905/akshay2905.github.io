OpenBudgetsIndia - Overview

OpenBudgetsIndia is India's first Open Data Platform for Budgets in India. The initiative aims to improve the fiscal transparency of budgets across different tiers of governments by improving the accessibility and understandability of Budget Data.

Successes and Impact of the initiative

  • Signed a MoU with AS-CFMS, Gov. of Assam as a knowledge partner bringing in the ground level policy changes. The policy change helped Assam become the most transparent states regarding budgetary practices. As part of the MoU, we undertook capacity building workshop for government officials to various topics related to Open Data, Open Source Solution, participatory budgeting, Data Analysis/Visualization Tools and much more. The MoU also lead to a collaborative project to build a public dashboard to help visualize budget data.
  • The platform has exhibited steady growth in different KPIs. 1.4L+ unique organic users have visited the platform with 38% bounce rate. - 10K+ datasets across different tiers of government are available on the platform.
  • Articles/Stories have been published in media houses, academic journals using budget dataset and visualizations. Ex - WHO References , Arcticle on healthcare issues in India

Responsibilities

I lead Design and Full Stack Development of different products of the OpenBudgetsIndia initiative. I was also responsible for building data pipelines to scrape, wrangle/munge, publish and visualize data on different products. Worked with an extremely small team and collaborated with different government agencies, civil societies, independent researchers and tech communities as part of the initiative. We built a range of products with little resources and tight deadlines.
